About Unjunk Me

UnJunk Me was born in New Jersey: Two Guys and a Junk Dream .

UnJunk Me began with the desire to offer more services and better ways to assist our clientele. UnJunk Me is a division of PestPlus Environmental Services.

PestPlus provides pest control and specializes in bed bug eradication. Part of that service is removing infested bedding and furniture. We wanted to address two issues: 1. No outside junk companies would handle anything with bed bugs, and 2. What do we do with the furniture when we remove it?

As a commercial business with commercial service vehicles, we do not have the same privilege as homeowners who can simply take their junk to the dump. There are rules we have to follow and licensing we have to apply for in order to be accepted.

Many of our clientsâ€™ homes also had junk and items no longer wanted by the homeowners. Plus, hoarder homes often have bug infestation issues.

Based on the needs of our clientele, and the 12 month licensing process in New Jersey, we were accepted and became licensed as a waste management company, in addition to the pest control company we have been operating for over 20 years.

UnJunk Me currently provides service only in New Jersey. We offer two types of services for commercial and residential needs: We can drop off a 20 yard container at your property and you can do everything yourself, or we can assist you and handle the entire project from start to finish.
